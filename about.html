About Me

I am a Graphic Designer and burgeoning Full Stack Developer based in Texas. I have over 10 years experience doing freelance graphic design and digital artwork utilizing Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. I also enjoy making traditional media art for home decor as well as design for apparel.

Graphic Design

Most of my design work previously was for myself or my personal network. In 2018 I decided that I would like to try to turn this passion into a side stream of income. I started submitting designs to Threadless and Bucketfeet.com with some sweet kicks that I designed. It was after this success that I decided I would start a portfolio online to showcase some of my works and skills that I have to contribute.

Full Stack Development
